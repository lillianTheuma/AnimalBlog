Fun Facts about Dogs:

  • Dogs do not appear in the 2016 animated film Zootopia, because dogs were selectively bred from wolves by humans, and humans never existed in the world of Zootopia.
  • Dogs do not just see in black and white. They are closest to red-green colorblind, and see the world in shades of yellow and blue!
  • All dogs are good dogs!
