Fun Facts about Dogs:

  1. Dogs do not appear in the 2016 animated film Zootopia. This is because dogs were selectively bred from wolves by humans, and humans never existed in the world of Zootopia.
  2. Contrary to popular belief, dogs do not just see in black and white. Their color spectrum is similar to the red-green colorblindness found in humans. They see the world in shades of yellow and blue!
  3. All dogs are good dogs!
